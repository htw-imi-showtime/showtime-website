{{<image src="images/overview2.png" alt="pages of the app">}}


**Dashboard**

Get a quick overview of your ongoing learning modules or weekly challenge, open language assessments and daily activities.




* **Milestones and Badges:**

Capture and celebrate every important step in your and your child's language development journey with Milestones and Badges. 




**Learning Modules**



Access short, interactive lessons that teach you how to support language learning in your everyday life.




**Challenges**

Discover playful weekly tasks that turn your daily routines into effective language-building moments.



{{<image src="images/learn_dash_challenge.png" alt="pages of the app">}}




**Progress**


* **Vocabulary Tracker:**

Track every new word or sentence your child learns and watch their personal digital dictionary grow.

* **Language Diary:**

Keep a personal journal of special moments and notes on your language journey.

* **Standardized Language Assessments:**

Complete standardized language assessments directly in the app to monitor developmental progress. 

{{<image src="images/progress2.png" alt="progress page">}}





**Family Access**

Language learning is a team effort. Invite your partner, grandparents or caregivers to join your family group on the app so everyone can contribute to and keep an eye on the child's language development.
